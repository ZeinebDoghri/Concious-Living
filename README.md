# 🌿 ConsciousPlate — AI-Powered Food Safety & Sustainability App

> **Every meal should be safer, smarter, and more conscious.**  
> ConsciousPlate is an AI-powered mobile application that unites food waste reduction, contamination detection, and personalized health guidance — all from a single photo.

---

## 📖 Overview

ConsciousPlate is built around the theme of **Conscious Living**. It tackles three of the most urgent challenges in modern food service:

| Challenge | What We Solve |
|---|---|
| 🗑️ **Food Waste** | Detect leftovers, monitor freshness, read expiry dates, sort compostables, optimize quantities |
| ⚠️ **Food Contamination** | Identify spoilage and contamination signs before food reaches the plate |
| 🥗 **Health & Nutrition** | Recognize dishes, flag allergens, and deliver personalized calorie and nutrient insights |

Designed for both **kitchen staff** and **customers**, ConsciousPlate is the first solution to bring sustainability, safety, and well-being into a single, beautiful mobile experience.

---

## ✨ Features

### ♻️ Waste Reduction
- **Leftover Detection** — Automatically detects remaining food on plates and in buffets
- **Freshness Monitoring** — Assesses the visual freshness of ingredients and dishes
- **Expiry Date Reader** — Reads product labels via OCR to trigger alerts before food becomes unsafe
- **Smart Portioning** — Helps kitchens prepare the right quantities, turning guesswork into precision
- **Compost Sorting** — Classifies waste into compostable and non-compostable categories

### 🛡️ Food Safety
- **Contamination Detection** — Flags visual signs of spoilage or contamination before food is served
- **Real-Time Alerts** — Instant notifications for kitchen teams when unsafe items are identified

### 💚 Health & Nutrition
- **Dish Recognition** — Identifies meals from a single photo
- **Allergen Detection** — Highlights allergens present in dishes in real time
- **Personalized Insights** — Provides calorie counts and nutrient breakdowns tailored to chronic conditions and dietary goals

## 📸 How It Works

1. **Capture** — The user takes a photo of a plate, buffet, dish, or product label
2. **Analyze** — Our AI pipeline processes the image using computer vision and deep learning
3. **Act** — Results are returned in seconds: freshness scores, allergen flags, expiry alerts, nutrition data, or waste classification
4. **Decide** — Kitchen staff and customers get clear, actionable insights directly in the app
